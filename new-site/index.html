DEPARTMENT OF DIGITAL ARTS

FACULTY OF MEDIA AND KNOWLEDGE SCIENCES

The Department of Digital Arts, at the Faculty of Media and Knowledge Sciences (MAKS), bridges contemporary artistic practice with electronic and digital technologies. Through our BFA, MFA and Ph.D programs, research at the Department considers the realities of current art and design practices that foster interdisciplinarity through the use of a diversity of media, ranging from the traditional, through to the most recent digital technologies. Its students are led to initiate, broaden and sustain a refined, critical and unique voice through developing a practice founded on informed experimentation, where value is given to contextual historical and theoretical frameworks, contemporary conceptualization and aesthetic articulation, and sound technical accomplishment.
