EXHIBITION 2022

Impression is an art exhibition finalising the three academic years of the students at the BFA of Digital Arts. Following these three long years spent in readjustment between the virtual and the physical, overcoming difficult circumstances, this exhibition marks the amalgamation of their growth. Featuring 21 different dissertation projects, ranging across a variety of themes and disciplines, this is now the lasting impression which they want to leave.

The exhibition was showcased on campus, held at the University of Malta on 20th and 21st May at University of Malta, displayed in the images above.
