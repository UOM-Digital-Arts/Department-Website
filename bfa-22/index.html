ABOUT

'Impression' is an exhibition that concludes the three academic years spent at the BFA of Digital Arts in readjustment between the virtual and the physical. After what seemed like an endless fever dream, the class persevered, adapting to and overcoming the strange circumstances that the world threw at us. We evolved over time, and this exhibition is a culmination of that evolution. This is the lasting impression we want to leave after zooming through technique and theory, absorbing trends and industry histories.
