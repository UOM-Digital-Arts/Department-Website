Jeanine Meli

jeaninemeli.com

Translating Poetry into Imagery through Illustration

Illustration

Jeanine’s project decodes the visual abstraction found in selected poetry and translates it into visual imagery through contemporary illustrations. The study is based on the interpretation and visualisation of poetry to create a modern style of drawings that contextualise the poetic concept. The ultimate aim is to enhance poetic concepts that capture the poet’s thoughts, to create a further ocular dimension to the original interaction of words. Poetic thoughts are translated and transcribed through digital illustrations.
