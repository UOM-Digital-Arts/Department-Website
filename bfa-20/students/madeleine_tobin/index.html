Evil Within - An Illustrated Guide to the Cambodian Genocide

My project shows, through the medium of illustration, a basic guide to the Cambodian Genocide and the lasting effects of the Khmer Rouge regime, explaining through graphics, the timeline of the crisis, and key points of life in Cambodia during this troubled time. My aim is to help raise awareness of the horrors witnessed during the Cambodian Genocide through illustration. My hope is that through this simple introduction viewers will become curious and want to learn more, not only about this particular event but about many forgotten wars and atrocities the world over. Bringing attention to war crimes and totalitarianism helps to prevent the mistakes of the past being repeated in the future.
