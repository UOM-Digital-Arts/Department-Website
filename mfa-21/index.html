We are Ennead: A group of nine students following the practice-based Masters of Fine Arts in Digital Arts Programme at the Department of Digital Arts, University of Malta. Our final year research projects explore the link between the analogue an the digital through our individual practices and backgrounds. We will be exhibited at Spazju Kreattiv from the 4th till 27th June 2021.
