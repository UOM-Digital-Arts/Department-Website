SINTESI IS AN EXHIBITION SHOWCASING THE CULMINATION OF THE BFA IN DIGITAL ARTS COURSE AT THE UNIVERSITY OF MALTA


THE TERM SINTESI REFLECTS THE PROCESS OF COMBINING ELEMENTS TO SYNTHESISE ART AND EXPRESS COMPLEX CONCEPTS. THE EXHIBITION CELEBRATES THE DIVERSE ARTISTIC ABILITIES OF THE STUDENTS, SHOWCASING PROJECTS IN FINE ARTS, PHOTOGRAPHY, GRAPHIC DESIGN, ILLUSTRATION, AND ANIMATION.
